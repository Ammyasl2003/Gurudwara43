GURDWARA SANT SEWA CHANDIGARH

LOCATION
GURDWARA SANT SEWA
SECTOR 43 A CHANDIGARH

Gurdwara Sant Sewa is a Sikh Temple located in CHANDIGARH. The Gurdwara was established in 1980, and have gone through several stages of developments over the years. It provides a place for activity and worship for the Sikh Community. All visitors are welcome to participate in the weekly Divans.
DIWAN SCHEDULE

Gurdwara is open everyday from 6:00AM-8:00PM. If you would like to perform a Shabad Kirtan during the Divan, please contact at the stage.
APPEAL

The Gurdwara Sahib Building is under construction. Generous donations from the local and international Sangat made commencement of the construction of the Gurdwara Sahib building possible. Since our area sangat is small compared to other mareas in Chandigarh. This appeal is to all the Khalsa Sangat in Chandigarh and abroad. Khalsa jee, you are earnestly requested to donate with open hearts to help accomplish this great undertaking to complete the Gurdwara Sahib building. All contributions are tax deductible. A proper receipt will be promptly provided for all contributions.
